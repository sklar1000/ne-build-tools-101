# Setup: Replit

**Time**: 5 minutes
**Cost**: Free (free tier is enough for pretotype testing)
**Best for**: Zero-setup builds, everything lives in your browser

---

## Step 1: Create an Account

1. Go to [replit.com](https://replit.com)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Use your Rice Google account (easiest) or any email
4. You're in. No software to install.

---

# WARNING: If you are on the free plan, DO NOT do the following before class. You will consume 100% of your free tokens.

## Step 2: Create Your First Project

1. Click **+ Create Repl** (top left)
2. Choose **HTML, CSS, JS** as the template (this works for quizzes, landing pages, calculators — most pretotype artifacts)
3. Name it something descriptive: `nutrition-quiz` or `compliance-checker`
4. Click **Create Repl**

**What just happened**: Replit created a project folder with three files — `index.html` (structure), `style.css` (styling), and `script.js` (logic). You don't need to touch these directly.

---

## Step 3: Use Replit Agent (the AI)

1. Look for the **AI chat panel** on the right side (or click the AI/Agent icon)
2. This is where you describe what you want to build
3. Type your description (be specific — see [What is AI Coding?](what-is-ai-coding.md) for how to write good prompts)

**Example first message:**

> Build me a web-based quiz for dog owners. It should ask 8 questions about their dog (breed, age, weight, activity level, current food, monthly budget, allergies, raw food experience). After answering, show a personalized nutrition score (1-10) with 3 recommendations. Collect their email at the end. Clean design, mobile-friendly, green and white colors.

4. Replit Agent will write the code and show you a **preview** in real-time
5. Look at the preview. Want changes? Just say them:
   - "Make the font bigger"
   - "Add a progress bar showing which question they're on"
   - "Change the scoring to show High/Medium/Low instead of numbers"

---

## Step 4: Test It

1. Click the **Run** button (green play button at the top)
2. Your project opens in a preview window inside Replit
3. Click through it as if you were a customer — does it work? Does it make sense?
4. Test on your phone: open the preview URL in your phone's browser

---

## Step 5: Deploy (Make It Live)

1. Click **Deploy** in the top menu
2. Choose **Static** deployment (free for simple web pages)
3. Replit gives you a **public URL** — anyone with this link can use your quiz/tool
4. Copy this URL — this is what you'll send to potential customers for testing

**What just happened**: Your project went from running on Replit's servers to being a real website that anyone in the world can visit. You now have a pretotype with a URL.

---

## Tips

- **Save often** — Replit auto-saves, but check that your changes are reflected in the preview
- **If something breaks**: Tell the AI "something broke, the quiz doesn't load anymore" — it will diagnose and fix
- **If you want to start over**: Create a new Repl rather than trying to fix a mess
- **If you need a database** (to save responses): Ask the AI "save quiz responses to a Google Sheet using a webhook" — this is simpler than setting up a database
- **Free tier limits**: You get limited compute time. For pretotype testing, this is more than enough. Don't upgrade unless you need to.
